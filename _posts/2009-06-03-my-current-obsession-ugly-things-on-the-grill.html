Of late, I'm obsessed with cooking somewhat ugly, but incredibly delicious, things on the grill. Turns out grilling pizzas isn't as hard as some would have you believe--I'm not a pro at it yet, but you can put together some dang good dinners with some Pillsbury pizza dough, spaghetti sauce, and the toppings of your choice. Now if I could just get them to not stretch out in such odd shapes when I put them on the grill--
